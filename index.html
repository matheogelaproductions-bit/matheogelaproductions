About Us

Matheogela Productions is an independent South African music publishing company founded in 2021. We work with independent artists on a song-by-song basis. We sign songs—not artists—helping creators release, protect, distribute and promote their music while keeping their independence.

About Matheogela Productions

Matheogela Productions is an independent South African music publishing company dedicated to helping songwriters, composers, producers, and independent artists protect, register, distribute, and monetize their music worldwide. We believe every great song deserves professional publishing, transparent royalty management, and global opportunities.

🎵

Our Story

Founded in 2021, Matheogela Productions was created to bridge the gap between talented independent creators and the professional music industry. Instead of signing artists, we work on a song-by-song basis, allowing creators to maintain ownership while we handle publishing administration and release support.

🚀

Our Mission

To empower independent creators by providing world-class music publishing, copyright protection, royalty administration, metadata management, and global distribution support.

🌍

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading independent music publishing company, connecting African music with audiences across the world while ensuring every songwriter receives the recognition and royalties they deserve.
