About Matheogela Productions

Matheogela Productions is an independent music publishing company dedicated to helping artists protect, register, distribute, and monetize their music worldwide.

We believe every song deserves professional publishing, accurate copyright management, and global exposure. We work with independent artists while allowing them to maintain ownership and creative freedom.

🎵 Mission

To empower independent artists through professional publishing, copyright protection, and global music distribution.

🌍 Vision

To become Africa's leading independent music publishing company connecting local talent with the global music industry.

⭐ Values

Integrity • Creativity • Transparency • Artist Development • Innovation

Why Choose Us?

Unlike traditional labels, we focus on songs—not artists. Our mission is to help independent creators keep their freedom while ensuring every song is properly managed, protected and positioned for success.
